MUSTBENCH

Benchmarking and Advancing Temporal Grounding in Music LLMs

Paper Code Dataset Demo

Overview

MUSTBENCH evaluates whether Music LLMs can identify not only what happens in music, but also when it happens. The benchmark includes five temporally grounded music QA tasks.

Tasks

Temporal Source Grounding (TSG)

Find when a specific instrument or vocal first appears or finally disappears.

Local Transition Recognition (LTR)

Select the musical change that occurs at a given timestamp.

Transition-Aware Description (TAD)

Describe the musical transition occurring around a timestamp.

Global Temporal Ordering (GTO)

Determine the chronological order of multiple musical transitions.

Mood Trajectory Reasoning (MTR)

Locate the highest or lowest emotional intensity intervals in a track.
